About Me

This is who I am

Hi there! My name is Jacob Steward, and I'm excited to get to know one another better. I come from the beautiful city of Provo, Utah, but have also had incredible opportunities to live all around the country, and even abroad for a time. I'm the youngest of six kids and family means everything to me. You can usually find me trying out a new recipe in the kitchen, catching up on sleep with a quick nap, or spending time with those that matter most to me.

I'm preparing to enter my junior year at Brigham Young University and am currently seeking admission at the Marriott School of Business. Some of my favorite classes and areas of study are Marketing, Experience Design, and Social Impact. Beyond the classroom I have loved gaining experience through hands on application. I have been priviliged to partner with Premium Ice Cream Co. as a marketing intern, and Dr. Sarah Coyne as a research assistant. I also currently teach Mandarin Chinese (普通话) at the Provo MTC to religious volunteers who will serve all over the world.

My vision for the future is simple: to change the world! That might sound a little cheesy, but here me out. Each individual truly does have the capacity to positively affect those around them. I want my simple daily actions to uplift my peers and benefit society. My goals to accomplish this vision are simple 1) Continue education to maximize knowledge, 2) Work with companies, organizations, and individuals with positive missions, and 3) Do good everyday. I've been given so much in my life, my only hope is to return that positivity.
